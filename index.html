About Public Healthcare

Public healthcare is the responsibility of the government to provide healthcare services to its citizens. This includes preventive care, such as vaccinations and screenings, as well as treatment for illnesses and injuries.

Public healthcare is important because it ensures that everyone has access to quality healthcare, regardless of their income or ability to pay.

Services Offered

  • Preventative care (vaccinations, screenings)
  • Treatment for illnesses and injuries
  • Maternal and child health services
  • Mental health services
  • Dental care

Public health organizations in Nigeria

  • The Nigerian Federal Ministry of Health
  • National Primary Health Care Development Agency
  • Nigerian Center for Disease Control
  • National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and control
  • Dental care
